Bison Burgers

Description

The burger one of the best food on planet earth. How could it possibly become better. I have an answer for you. All you have to do is add bison. Welcome to the bison burger.

The bison is not only offers a superior taste over ground beef, but also has more micro-nutrients. Add some onion rings and you have an amazing burger.

Ingrediants

  • Ground Bison
  • Buns
  • Tomatoes
  • Lettuce
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Shape the Bison into a patty shape
  2. Season Bison with salt and black pepper
  3. Cook on the grill till the right temp.
  4. Put bison on one bun
  5. Add desired topping and enjoy
