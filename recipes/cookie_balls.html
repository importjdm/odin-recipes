Cookie Balls

Description

A cookie ball is a tasty treat that any one would love. It's a cookie that is in chape of a ball, ooh how fun! The kids are sure to love it and so is your husband.

You can find cookie balls in stores all over, but the best ones are the homemade ones. Our recipe is among the best and is sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chocolate sandwich cookies crushed
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
  • 1 pound vanilla flavored candy

Steps

  1. Get a large bowl out
  2. Put all the crushed cookies in bowl
  3. Now add the cream cheese in the bowl
  4. Roll into balls
  5. With fork dip into candy coating
