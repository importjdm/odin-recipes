Pancakes

Description

The following food is a fluffy cake like dish tha is meant to be eaten in the morning. This dish is served in many restraunt around the country. It is also very simple to make

The dish originated in the middle east probably, I'm not sure. You can add fruits like bananas, strawberries, or even blackberries to the dish. If fruit isn't your thing you can even add chocolate chips or even penut butter.

Ingredients

  • Pancake mix
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • chocolate chips
    •

    Steps

    1. Add pancake mix into bowl with the milk
    2. Add the eggs and mix till no clumps
    3. Add chocolate chips and butter
    4. Dump batter into hot pan
    5. Cook and enjoy
